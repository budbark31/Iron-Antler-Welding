About Brayden Lewis

Brayden Lewis is an entrepreneur and skilled welder with 5 years of total experience, including 2 years of formal welding education and 3 years of specialized work on military vehicles. His training has equipped him with the precision and discipline required for high-performance welding under demanding conditions.

As the driving force behind Iron Antler Mobile Welding, Brayden combines his passion for metalwork and whitetail deer hunting with a commitment to quality, offering expert mobile welding for agricultural, construction, and trailer needs. Focused, reliable, and always ready to get the job done right the first time.
